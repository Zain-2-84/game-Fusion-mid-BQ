The Finals.

Screenshots



Description:


The Finals is a free-to-play first-person shooter, developed and published by Nexon subsidiary Embark Studios. The game focuses on team-based matches on maps with a destructible environment, where players are encouraged to use the dynamic environment to their advantage.


Game mods:


Singleplayer mod:


Single player is available, where you can explore maps, battle against bots, and practice shooting or other game mechanics.

Multiplayer mod:


It is explicitly a game about exchanging death for money, where you compete in teams for virtual cash prizes, pay real money to give your tracksuit-wearing competitor a snazzier outfit, and awesome environmental destruction.
